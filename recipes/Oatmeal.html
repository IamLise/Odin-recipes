Description

Oatmeal is a nice and tasty breakfast dish that is easy to make. It is a perfect dish for cold mornings in the winter.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix
  2. Boil

Oatmeal

1. Mix 1 dl of oats with 2 dl of water.

2. Let it boil in a pot while stiring until it has the right texture.

3. Enjoy!
